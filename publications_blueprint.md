# Publications Blueprint

## Goal
Create a premium academic publications page that is easy to scan, feels prestigious, and remains usable even with a large volume of entries.

## Interaction
Use expandable accordion sections.
Each section should open smoothly and preserve layout stability.

## Visual style
- clean white background
- elegant dividers
- small teal accents
- strong typographic hierarchy
- year chips / tags optional
- subtle hover states

## Required categories
1. Cărți și volume coordonate
2. Capitole de carte internaționale
3. Capitole și contribuții naționale
4. Articole științifice
5. Ghiduri și recomandări
6. Abstracte și prezentări la congrese

## Starter examples that should be surfaced prominently
### Cărți și volume coordonate
- 2022 — Pregnancy with Artificial Intelligence. A 9,5 months journey from preconception to birth. Springer Nature.
- 2016 — Essential in Colposcopy.
- 2016 — Ultrasound in Gynecology.
- 2016 — Ultrasound in Obstetrics. Atlas.
- 2022 — Obstetrica.
- 2022 — Ginecologia.
- 2024 — Inovation in Obstetrics and Gynecology.

### Capitole de carte internaționale
Include curated entries from Springer / InTechOpen and related books.

### Ghiduri și recomandări
Highlight ISUOG translations / editorial contributions and national guidelines contributions.

## Item structure
- Title
- Year
- Publisher / journal / book
- Optional DOI / link icon
- Optional compact subtitle

## Important
Do not try to dump the entire CV unformatted.
Use a structured content source and elegant grouping.
