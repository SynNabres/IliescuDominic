# Project Context

## Goal
Create a premium, high-conversion personal medical website for Prof. Univ. Dr. Dominic Iliescu that increases appointment bookings and reinforces trust, authority, and scientific credibility.

## Positioning
The website should present Dr. Iliescu as:
- specialist in obstetrics and gynecology
- physician with strong authority in maternal-fetal medicine and advanced ultrasound
- academic and scientific figure, not just a clinician
- trusted premium choice in Craiova for women seeking clear, modern, evidence-based care

## Why this site should exist
The current visibility of Dr. Iliescu is nested within the clinic website and is too transactional.
The new site should better capture:
- branded search intent
- premium personal authority
- patient trust before booking
- direct appointment conversion

## Audience
Primary:
- women in Craiova and surrounding areas seeking an OB-GYN specialist
- pregnant patients seeking advanced evaluation and ultrasound
- patients comparing physicians before booking

Secondary:
- patients searching directly for Dominic Iliescu
- patients seeking colposcopy, gynecologic evaluation, postpartum follow-up, or infertility-related counseling
- professional and academic audiences validating authority

## Key trust pillars
- professor status
- hospital and university affiliations
- publication history
- committee and society involvement
- years of clinical and academic activity
- transparent services and prices
- easy contact and booking

## Page set
Only:
- Acasă
- Despre
- Servicii
- Activitate academică
- Contact
- Publicații

## Important content rule
The site must speak directly to patients.
It must not read like a CV dump.
It must not read like a clinic brochure.
It must not read like a template.

## Design direction
Visually related to GinEcho:
- white
- soft neutrals
- teal / blue-green accents
- elegant and clean
- premium medical feel
- calm motion
- strong typography
- modern cards
