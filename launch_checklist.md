# Launch Checklist

## Content
- All visible text is in Romanian
- No placeholders remain
- No self-referential/meta copy remains
- Services and prices match approved source
- Contact information verified
- Academic claims reviewed

## UX
- Mobile sticky CTA works
- CTA buttons visible across pages
- Services page scans easily on mobile
- Contact form is functional
- Publications accordions are smooth and accessible

## SEO
- Unique page titles
- Unique meta descriptions
- Clean slugs
- Schema validates
- Sitemap generated
- OG tags render properly

## Technical
- Lighthouse mobile performance acceptable
- Images optimized
- No broken links
- No hydration errors
- Forms validated
- Maps/links working

## Final review
- Tone feels premium, calm, authoritative
- Design feels aligned with GinEcho family, but more personal
- Site reads like a real medical brand, not a template
